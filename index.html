About Northline

Local detailing with a focus on quality, presentation, and trust.

Northline Auto & Surfaces is a local auto detailing and surface-care business serving Kelowna and nearby areas. We offer both shop-based and mobile detailing services, giving clients a convenient way to get their vehicle cleaned, corrected, protected, and professionally finished.

Every vehicle is different, which is why quotes are based on photos and condition. A lightly used sedan, a family SUV, and a heavily used work truck all require different levels of time, product, and attention.

Our goal is not to rush through a quick clean. Our goal is to provide a professional detailing experience with careful prep, clean results, honest communication, and a finish that makes your vehicle look properly cared for.

FAQ

Questions people ask before booking.

Do I need to send photos before booking?

Yes. Photos help confirm the vehicle’s size, condition, and problem areas so your quote is accurate before the appointment.

Do you offer mobile detailing and shop appointments?

Yes. Northline Auto & Surfaces offers both shop-based appointments and mobile detailing when location, parking, weather, and access are suitable.

Why are prices listed as starting prices?

Vehicle size, condition, and required labour can vary. Heavy pet hair, stains, mud, salt, smoke odor, and neglected interiors may require additional time.

How does the 30% new client offer work?

New clients receive 30% off their first three eligible detailing appointments when booking an Interior & Exterior Detailing package, Paint Correction & Protection package, or Polish Service.

Can I book ceramic coating or paint correction immediately?

You can request it, but paint services require a condition review first. The final quote depends on paint condition, vehicle size, and the level of correction or protection needed.

Do you clean work trucks or heavily used vehicles?

Yes. Heavily used vehicles can be detailed, but excessive mud, debris, pet hair, staining, or job-site buildup may affect the final price.

Can you detail at condos or workplaces?

Usually, as long as there is permission, safe access, and enough room to work. Send the location details when requesting your quote.
